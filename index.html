The End-to-End Operating System for AI Procurement

Based on the rigorous IEEE P3119-2025 standard, our platform provides a structured, repeatable process for responsible AI adoption.

Align AI Projects with Business Goals from Day 1

Move beyond vague objectives. Our guided workflows and 'Problem Definition Canvas' ensure every AI initiative is tied to a clear business need, with all stakeholders aligned before a single dollar is spent.

  • Define the problem, not just the technology.
  • Map all impacted stakeholders, including vulnerable groups.
  • Ensure the 'why' is as clear as the 'what'.

Automate Risk Assessment & Ensure Compliance

Our dynamic engine classifies AI systems by risk level, tailoring due diligence accordingly. Maintain a complete, auditable record that demonstrates compliance with emerging standards and regulations.

  • Automatically assess technical, ethical, and operational risks.
  • Track and manage risks in a centralized register.
  • Generate compliance reports based on the IEEE P3119-2025 standard.

Go Beyond the Sales Pitch with Vendor Intelligence

Stop relying on vendor claims. Use our standardized questionnaires and rubrics to get an objective, data-driven view of vendor maturity, security posture, and ethical governance.

  • Leverage 14 key vendor maturity questions.
  • Compare potential suppliers with automated 'Vendor Scorecards'.
  • Maintain a central database of all vendor interactions and evaluations.

Interrogate Every Aspect of the AI System

Our platform guides you through 44 critical system evaluation questions. Dig deep into data provenance, model assumptions, potential biases, and training methodologies with a structured and collaborative workflow.

  • Review all vendor documentation in one place.
  • Collaborate with legal, IT, and business teams using annotation tools.
  • Create a defensible record of your due diligence process.

Build Accountability with Ironclad Contracting

Use our library of pre-approved, standards-aligned contract clauses to ensure accountability is baked into every agreement. Define KPIs and monitor performance long after the deal is signed.

  • Access templates for data rights, transparency, and auditability.
  • Define and track performance KPIs in a monitoring dashboard.
  • Ensure clear lines of accountability if the system fails.

Gain Real-Time, C-Suite Visibility

Empower your leadership with a board-ready dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of your entire AI portfolio, including risk exposure, ROI, and compliance status.

  • View all AI procurement projects in a single dashboard.
